Covid Yodha

"Small acts of kindness when multiplied by millions of people can transform the world."

Covid YODHA is an NGO which is determined to choose humanity and believes that the sole meaning of life is to serve others in need. It is with this agenda,that we've built up a team and more than that,a family, to help us succeed in this mission of serving mankind. Even though this pandemic brought out our strength, abilities and skills, we even saw how it uprooted the life of many, and left them hopeless. Thus, what we aim to do is to provide everyone around us with the support that is necessary during these unprecedented times, and give them a ray of hope to not give up. We welcome you into our family of Covid YODHA and we hope that together, we can overcome this darkness and bring light into our world again.

What We Do?

The COVID-19 pandemic has greatly affected all of society, including teams in organizational settings. Collaborative teamwork is particularly susceptible to pandemic disruptions, as coordination across individuals becomes challenging in socially distanced and virtual contexts. So we at CovidYodha are here with our most sedulous care to provide with the information and necessities which help bridging the gap between knowledge and action; with:

Informative blogs concerning subjects (doubts/ myths/information) regarding Covid-19.

Podcasts and interviews with our respected frontline workers.

We help you find the verified leads for anything in need, be it - oxygen cylinders, bed availability, medicines, plasma donors and many more resources.

Actively trying to solve your doubts regarding COVID-19 on our social media platforms.

We are here with our efforts to help all the people across our nation in an arduous journey. We will be grateful and welcoming for your volunteering with us to make our world better again.

OUR VISION:
“UNITED TO BEAT COVID-19”
We at Covid YODHA strongly believe that with integrity, consciousness and constructive mindset, we will triumph to exterminate COVID-19.
OUR MISSION:
Making India "MASK-FREE"
Helping in escalating the awareness and positivity during the uncertain times.
Assisting people needlessly in any way possible.
Take the edge of the fallacies and misconceptions pertaining to the virus.
We have been providing food resources to the needy people in lockdown and also working on procurement of medical resources in many cities.Our journey to serve mankind will not halt till the extermination of COVID-19.
